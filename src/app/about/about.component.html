Classroot Tech - About Us

We care a lot about computing, and we care a lot about equity.

That’s why we we created classroot, a web application to help teachers make their CS classrooms as inclusive as possible.

Many efforts to strengthen the STEM pipeline exist — but none have focused on a simple but important factor: classroom environment. Research shows that stereotypically “tech-bro” classrooms make girls less likely to consider taking computer science classes. That’s why classroot provides an easy, encouraging way for teachers to assess their classrooms and curricular efforts toward inclusivity.
